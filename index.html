About us

Vikram Roller Flour Mills Limited is among the pioneers of Agro-products manufacturing, specialized in the best quality of wheat products manufacturing. Vikram roller flour mills were incepted four decades back in 1973 in National Capital – New Delhi. In these glorious 48 years, the company has grown as one of the leading manufacturing of wheat products like – Wheat Flour (Atta), White Flour (Maida), Semolina (Suji), Bran (Chokar) and Broken Wheat (Dallia), etc.
